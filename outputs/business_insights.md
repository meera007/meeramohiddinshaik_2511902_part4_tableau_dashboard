
# Calculated Fields – Business Insights

## 1. Profit Margin
**Formula:** Profit / Sales  
**Insight:**  
Measures how efficiently the business converts revenue into profit.  
Low or negative margins indicate pricing or discount issues.

---

## 2. Cost
**Formula:** Sales - Profit  
**Insight:**  
Represents the estimated cost of goods sold.  
Used to analyze cost-heavy products and categories.

---

## 3. Average Order Value (AOV)
**Formula:** Total Sales / Number of Orders  
**Insight:**  
Indicates how much revenue each order generates on average.  
Helps evaluate customer spending behavior and pricing strategy.

---

## 4. Return Rate
**Formula:** Returned Orders / Total Orders  
**Insight:**  
Measures the percentage of returned orders.  
High return rates may indicate product quality or customer satisfaction issues.

---

## 5. Shipping Delay Bucket
**Logic:** Categorizes delivery days into buckets  
**Buckets:**
- Same Day
- Fast (1–2 days)
- Standard (3–5 days)
- Delayed (6+ days)

**Insight:**  
Helps analyze delivery performance and customer experience.  
Identifies operational inefficiencies in logistics.

# Business Insights from Sales Dashboard

This document summarizes key insights derived from the dashboard, including observations, supporting data evidence, interpretations, and recommended actions.

---

## 1. Sales Trend Insight

**Observation:**  
Sales show an overall upward trend over time with fluctuations across months.

**Data Evidence:**  
The Sales Trend line chart shows increasing total sales from 2024 to 2025, with noticeable peaks in certain months.

**Business Interpretation:**  
The business is growing, but demand is not consistent throughout the year, indicating seasonality.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Identify peak months and replicate successful campaigns  
- Investigate low-performing months for demand gaps  
- Plan inventory and marketing around seasonal trends  

---

## 2. Regional Performance Insight

**Observation:**  
Western and Northern regions generate higher sales compared to other regions.

**Data Evidence:**  
Regional map/bar chart shows higher sales volume and profit concentration in states like Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Delhi.

**Business Interpretation:**  
These regions have stronger customer demand and better market penetration.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Increase investment in high-performing regions  
- Analyze underperforming regions for growth opportunities  
- Expand distribution and marketing in weaker regions  

---

## 3. Category & Sub-category Profitability Insight

**Observation:**  
Some sub-categories generate high sales but low or negative profit.

**Data Evidence:**  
Category profitability chart shows certain sub-categories (e.g., tables/bookcases) with negative profit values.

**Business Interpretation:**  
High revenue does not guarantee profitability due to high costs or discount levels.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Review pricing and cost structure for loss-making products  
- Optimize product portfolio by reducing low-margin items  
- Focus on high-profit categories like Technology  

---

## 4. Customer Segment Behavior Insight

**Observation:**  
Corporate and Consumer segments contribute most to total sales.

**Data Evidence:**  
Bar chart shows higher sales and profit values for Corporate and Consumer segments compared to Home Office.

**Business Interpretation:**  
These segments are key revenue drivers and should be prioritized.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Create targeted offers for high-value segments  
- Analyze purchase behavior differences between segments  
- Improve engagement for the Home Office segment  

---

## 5. Discount Impact Insight

**Observation:**  
Higher discounts are often associated with lower or negative profit.

**Data Evidence:**  
Scatter plot shows a downward trend where profit decreases as discount increases beyond ~20%.

**Business Interpretation:**  
Excessive discounting is eroding profitability.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Limit high discount levels  
- Introduce controlled discount strategies  
- Evaluate profitability thresholds before applying discounts  

---

## 6. Shipping & Delivery Performance Insight

**Observation:**  
Longer delivery times are associated with lower sales performance.

**Data Evidence:**  
Shipping delay bucket chart shows that “Delayed (6+ days)” has lower sales compared to faster delivery categories.

**Business Interpretation:**  
Customers prefer faster delivery, impacting purchasing decisions.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Optimize logistics to reduce delivery time  
- Promote faster shipping options  
- Monitor shipping performance by region  

---

## 7. Return Pattern Insight

**Observation:**  
Certain categories and customer segments have higher return rates.

**Data Evidence:**  
Return analysis chart shows higher return rates in specific categories and some regions.

**Business Interpretation:**  
Returns may be driven by product quality issues, incorrect expectations, or delivery problems.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Investigate reasons for returns (product quality, shipping damage)  
- Improve product descriptions and images  
- Focus on reducing returns in high-risk areas  

---

## 8. Business Risk & Opportunity Insight

**Observation:**  
There is a combination of high discounts, negative profits, and returns in specific product areas.

**Data Evidence:**  
- Negative profits in some sub-categories  
- High discounts linked to losses  
- Return_flag present in multiple transactions  

**Business Interpretation:**  
This creates a financial risk but also an opportunity to improve efficiency and profitability.

**Recommended Action:**  
- Reduce dependency on discount-driven sales  
- Identify and fix loss-making products  
- Strengthen quality control and pricing strategy  

---
